Richard Morse


Hi, hope you're well!

I currently work as a Data Scientist at Boston Consulting Group, where I have performed advanced analytics and digital modeling work for clients ranging across the consumer, energy, and travel & tourism industries. At BCG, I am lucky to be able to combine my passions for building robust models and for implementing high performance systems.

While at Boston Consulting Group, and earlier in Rice University's Cohan Research Lab, I have employed big data analytics, mathematical optimization and distributed high-performance computing. This work has resulted in efficient data models with wide-ranging applications to sustainability, consumer personalization and healthcare.

Broadly, I am passionate about using mathematical analysis to make thoughtful decisions from big data. I'm an excellent communicator and a resourceful problem solver dedicated to helping a variety of organizations understand and optimize their choices.
